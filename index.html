Welcome to the magical world of My Melody, the sweetest little bunny from Mariland! This adorable white rabbit with her signature pink hood has been spreading joy and happiness since 1975. My Melody loves to bake cookies, especially almond pound cake, and she's always ready to help her friends with a warm heart and gentle smile. Her innocent charm and kind nature make her one of the most beloved characters in the Sanrio family. Join us as we explore her wonderful world filled with friendship, adventure, and lots of kawaii moments that will make your heart flutter with happiness!
