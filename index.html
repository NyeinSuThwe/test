Welcome to My Melody's World

Welcome to the enchanting world of My Melody, Sanrio's beloved white rabbit who has been spreading joy since 1975. With her signature pink hood crafted by her grandmother, My Melody represents kindness, friendship, and the simple pleasures of life in the magical Forest of Mariland.

Join us as we explore her wonderful world filled with heartwarming adventures, delicious baking sessions, and meaningful friendships that teach us valuable lessons about compassion and community.

About My Melody

My Melody is a charming white rabbit born in the Forest of Mariland on January 18th. She's known for her gentle personality, love of baking, and her treasured pink hood that never leaves her side. Her favorite treat is almond pound cake, which she loves to share with friends.

Character Profile

  • Birthday: January 18th
  • Birthplace: Forest of Mariland
  • Favorite Food: Almond Pound Cake
  • Hobbies: Baking & Reading
  • Special Item: Pink Hood from Grandma
  • Best Friend: Flat the Mouse

What makes My Melody truly special is her caring nature and ability to bring out the best in everyone she meets. Her adventures teach us about friendship, courage, and finding magic in everyday moments.
